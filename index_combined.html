Emergency SOS

GPS-tagged emergency alert via Telegram

EMERGENCY
⚠ FOR GENUINE EMERGENCIES ONLY
Sends your live GPS coordinates to the Telegram emergency contact with a direct Google Maps navigation link.
To find your ID, start @GetMyChatID_Bot or @userinfobot. Make sure you also start our bot @risksosbot to receive alerts.
📍 Location auto-detected via browser GPS
How It Works
  1. Click the SOS button above
  2. Allow location permission in your browser
  3. GPS coordinates are transmitted to the Telegram emergency bot
  4. A Google Maps link enables responders to navigate directly to you
  5. Remain at your location if safe to do so
